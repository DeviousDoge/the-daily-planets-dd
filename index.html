Sometimes, Earth can seem like a bit too much. It seems today that most news is bad news, or even just news we don't really want to hear about. That said, we are not advocates of distraction from current or past events. At the Daily Planets, we have found a solution to this predicament. If life on this planet seems overwhelming, why not supplement the days headlines with imagery from a distant, yet familiar planet? Introducing the Daily Planets, Earths first multi-planetary independent media source. (Possibly. We lack enough evidence to confidently claim this title, sadly. We need more funding for that.)
